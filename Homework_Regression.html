  1. An interpretation in context of both the slope and the intercept.

After doing a linear regression on the plot it was visible to see that the slope of this scatterplot is positive. Generally as the Freedom of a country increased the Happiness score in that country increased. There were some outliers that don’t follow this trend but still don’t affect the line.

The slope is 4.3174 which means that in order to get a Freedom score of one so in other words for every .1 Freedom score is .43 to be with the scale in our plot.

The intercept is 3.5252. So when the country has basically around 0 Freedom rating the score of Happiness is 3.5 which is not drastically low compared to the freedom rating.
